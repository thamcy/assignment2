Essential Tools

Grades is a distinction used by the manufacturer, Bandai, to describe generally how detailed a model is. Many models are available in multiple grades, depeicting the same fictional machine with different levels of detail.

Models within a grade are generally the same scale, as seen in the tabs below. However, the scale is not consistent 100% of the time although it is generally a good gauge.

Clippers

Clippers are an essential aspect to many different kinds of model building. It is used to seperate parts cleanly with minimal damage to any of the parts. A strong pair of scissors can replace this if it is unavailable, but it will not be as cleanly cut in many cases.

Craft Knife

Craft knives are important tools to carfully carve parts with greater accuracy. It is especially important when you have a weaker pair of clippers. Craft knives help to cut the excess material off of component pieces as well, which can be used after clipping to create a nice and smootly cut product.

Note: Be careful not to cut too much material as it might ruin your component pieces!

File

Files are used to smoothen out cut edges caused by clippers and craft knives. For certain types of model kits, files are also required to slightly alter the shapes of component pieces to allow them to fit together snugly. This is seen especially often in the woodcraft construction kits.

Tweezers

Tweezers are used for intricate design work. They are especially useful for actions such as sticker pasting and small piece-gluing. While they might seem to be not as essential as the other three tools, tweezers are incredibly important in enhancing the details of your model to make it that much more spectacular.

Optional

Some additional tools can make your models shine with even greater detail and realistic colours. For Gunpla in particular, the following three items are widely and commonly used in the making and finishing of models.

Markers for drawing lines and creating depth.

Matte spray paint to create a more rugged and realistic colour.

A cutting mat to do your craft with greater accuracy and detail, while protecting the floor or table.
